About Us

Who we are and what we do.

YokoOno Talent Agency Pvt. Ltd. is a talent management and booking agency specializing in finding the freshest live talents out there. We book tours, venues, campuses, festivals and club nights, while working closely with brands, labels, licensors, distributors and event agencies- thus building up and accelerating artist careers through performances, appearances and endorsements. We envision to become the most trusted booking agency in this part of the world, providing quality service to both our talents and our clients. We believe in transparency and quality, and promise to make your entertainment events much more exciting and happening! And yes, our BANDs do have an EDGE over others!

"The 'Business of Art' is very different from the 'Art Of Business'. In todays' digital era, successful artists are those who know both. We happen to understand both the fields to quite some extent, hence we try to bring our education and experience to work by supporting talents whom we believe in. We are confident of growing symbiotically and respectfully, if we look at things with a long term perspective, and work with honesty, integrity and a masterplan."
